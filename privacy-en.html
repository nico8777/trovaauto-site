Privacy Policy – Trova Auto Free

This app uses the camera and precise location only to save and find your parking spot. All data is stored only on your device and is not sent to external servers.

You can manually share photos/locations through the “Share” menu. Saved content can be deleted at any time from within the app.

Advertising

Trova Auto Free displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob and its advertising partners. These services may collect anonymous information, such as the device advertising ID, to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

You can adjust your preferences for personalized ads in your device’s Google settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

Permissions used

  • Camera
  • Location (foreground only)
  • Network status (to check connectivity and display ads)
