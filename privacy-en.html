Privacy Policy – Trova Auto Free

This app uses the camera and precise location only to save and find your parking spot. All data remains only on your device and is not sent to external servers.

You can manually share photos and location via the “Share” menu. Saved content can be deleted at any time from the app.

Advertising

Trova Auto Free displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob and its advertising partners. These services may collect anonymous information, such as the device’s advertising ID, to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

You can manage your preferences regarding personalized ads through Google settings on your device (Settings → Google → Ads).

Permissions used

  • Camera
  • Location (foreground only)
  • Network status (to check connectivity and show ads)
